How we met

Adriana and Samir met the new fashioned way…through an online dating app! :D Samir had just moved to California and started chatting with Adriana less than a month from moving in. After a few conversations, they decided to go on their first date in San Jose’s Japantown.

They spent their time chatting over mochi, llamas, troll fest (yes, that’s a thing), traveling, movies, and much more. The rest was history and the start of a great adventure.

Adriana and Samir have enjoyed spending the last 5 year traveling the world together and experiencing new places, foods, and cultures. They have visited 8 countries together: Japan, Iceland, Mexico, Canada, Germany, Belgium, Denmark, and Peru. They hope to continue traveling the world together embarking on new adventures and making many more memories.

Events

4th September

Mehndi 7PM - 12AM

Mehndi or henna is an ancient form of temporary body art. This is a traditional pre-wedding ceremony where mehndi is applied to the bride’s hands and feet. During our version of this event, the bride and groom's families will celebrate together, while a mehndi artist applies henna designs to whoever would like one. This will be a celebration with people wearing vibrant clothes, dancing, singing, and eating!

Wedding 4PM-10PM

There will be two wedding ceremonies, the Indian and Western, so brace yourselves! For the Indian, the bride and groom circle the sacred fire and are finally declared husband and wife.
